AVENGERS

The Avengers are a superhero team from Marvel Comics who protect the world. They have members like Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, and Captain America. They've appeared in comics, movies, and TV shows.

INFINITY WAR

"Infinity War" is a Marvel film where the Avengers battle Thanos, who wants to collect powerful stones for destructive purposes. It has an ensemble cast and sets up the events of "Avengers: Endgame."

ENDGAME

"Endgame" is the final chapter of the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. The Avengers try to undo Thanos' destruction and save the universe. It's a highly successful film known for its epic conclusion and record-breaking box office success.

THANOS

Thanos is a powerful supervillain in Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). He seeks to collect the Infinity Stones to wipe out half of all life in the universe and bring balance. Thanos is known for his purple skin, distinctive chin, and immense strength. He is a major antagonist in the MCU's "Avengers: Infinity War" and "Avengers: Endgame" films.

THOR

Thor is a superhero based on Norse mythology. He possesses superhuman strength and wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he is played by Chris Hemsworth and is a member of the Avengers.

SPIDER-MAN

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in "Amazing Fantasy #15" in 1962, published by Marvel Comics. Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is one of Marvel's most popular and enduring characters.
